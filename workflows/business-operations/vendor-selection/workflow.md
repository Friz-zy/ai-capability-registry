# Vendor Selection Workflow

Workflow id: `vendor-selection`

Use this workflow to compare vendors, SaaS tools, APIs, cloud services, or contractors and produce a recommendation.

## Files

- Manifest: `workflows/business-operations/vendor-selection/workflow.yaml`
- Common schemas: `workflows/templates/schemas/`
- Common artifact templates: `workflows/templates/artifacts/`

## Coordinator Notes

This workflow should compare options against requirements rather than preferences. Use `integration-development` after a vendor is selected and integration delivery begins.

Follow the manifest for stage order, role delegation, gates, and acceptance criteria.
