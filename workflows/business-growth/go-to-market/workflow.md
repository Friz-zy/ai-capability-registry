# Go To Market Workflow

Workflow id: `go-to-market`

Use this workflow to prepare launch strategy, messaging, channels, sales enablement, support readiness, and launch checklist.

## Files

- Manifest: `workflows/business-growth/go-to-market/workflow.yaml`
- Common schemas: `workflows/templates/schemas/`

## Coordinator Notes

This workflow assumes the product or launch scope is defined enough to prepare market-facing execution. Use `idea-validation` or `product-strategy` when product-market assumptions are still too weak.

Follow the manifest for stage order, role delegation, gates, and acceptance criteria.
